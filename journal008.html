JOURNAL #008

After walking through pure nothingness and studying the TV I finally found a place to stay.

It was a huge green planet covered all over with yellow leaves and giant white branches, it even had
a pink moon as a company but it sure had a weird rotation through its orbit. I am not one to discuss
astronomy and besides that doesn’t matter because I had to check if there was any kind of life in it.

I took a look at the branches and all of its leaves were dying one by one, it makes me wonder how
much time has this plant stayed alive. Everything was covered in yellow leaves and since I have an
artistic point of view this needs to be painted and put in a museum, its beauty is unimaginable,
however I’ve noticed how incredibly quiet this place is even though its all filled with cities.

There were buildings, vehicles, stations and even highways, but no cheese mac, this whole planet is
empty. I don’t know if these yellow leaves have anything to do with the absence of people, but that
sure sounds like an incredibly stupid excuse of extinction, unless all of these buildings were made by
one single person just to make it more awesome looking, that’s even stupider and egocentric.

However one thing that I've noticed is that the TV was shining a little bit, that has to mean that its
related with this planet. Anyways I am off to search even further but I am not positive of finding
anything new than just nothing.

Hopefully I will finally get some answers at last.
