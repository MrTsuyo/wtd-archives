JOURNAL #001

Voidness, emptiness, blankness.

Those were three of the first things I saw once I regained my consciousness, pure nothingness
covered in a beautiful blue cosmos of which a colossal red star kept him company, that was the next
thing I noticed, it was beautiful and warm, really warm, but I tried to ignore it to focus on what
happened before I lost my conscience.

I tried to remember anything that happened before waking up but I have mixed memories, like
being a kid that cutted himself with a shard, a green being that’s giving a ring to someone, a
shadowy figure that betrays an old man and...

I got the feeling none of those memories are from my property, but I still cannot understand why I
am sharing this twisted memories with different people. After wondering about my own existence, I
couldn't help but notice I showed up in the middle of a destroyed satellite, red fragments were
scattered around me like if I was the one who caused all this, maybe it is an explanation of why I am
here? I guess I'll never know.

Suddenly I noticed something that caught my attention, it was a broken gray television that was
wandering in this ocean of stars like me, it was covered in blood and it seemed as if someone had
been hit on the head with it, I couldn't help but be tempted to pick it up and try to fix it to see if it
got any answers for your existance.

And that’s when I found an objective for me.
