TAPE_NJR: THE PRINCESS AND THE SPACE PIRATE

Once upon a time…





There was a princess

Who fell in love with a Space Pirate

And they both agreed they were meant to be together.

Even if the world told them, it was an impossible love





But the pirate had a plan, to get a treasure from a faraway land

That will bond their love for the rest of their lives.



At the end of the season, the pirate's quest was successful

But the pirate wasn’t himself anymore

Saying that she had a power in her tears that he sought



The pirate trapped the princess, she was a princess no more

Surrounded by a thousand bubbles that she cried everyday





But her magic within her tears wasn’t good enough

Filled with anger from his broken promise of bonding love,

Instead transformed him into a monster…
