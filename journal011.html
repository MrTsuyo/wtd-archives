JOURNAL #011

Well after looking for hours I ate my own words, there IS actually somebody in here, the only life i
could ever find in this abandoned planet, she is a gorgeous gray lady with large horns, red make up
and with some kind of kimono on, she is meditating in... a pretty peculiar way, and she didn’t look
like she was gonna answer me any moment.

The TV led me to her, it seems this can act like a metal detector. And by then I thought the TV was
from her since it was brighter when i got closer, but seeing her lack of interest in anything else
kinda made it clear that she wasn’t the reason.

There was a note close to her though and it said that I could own the building that is below her.
That’s the nicest thing anyone has ever done to me!

The first person I've ever met and I already make a friend! I wish there was some more interaction
than just a single note, like making jokes, talking about videogames, maybe dating? Is that how
people socialize? I have a memory that tells me that that’s just how you start a relationship, but the
other memory just tells me that if you like somebody you should put a ring on it and never question
it. At this point I just don’t listen to those memories so whatever.

Now I have a whole building of my own where I can keep doing my working stuff! It feels like it was
10 years ago since I regain my consciousness, and now I have a home. Now I know that it was this
place the reason why the TV was shining so much, so now I am off to make a tour through all my
new house.

Hopefully this leads me to something good.
