 Chapter 1




Once upon a time there was an old woman who lived in the middle of the jungle inhabited by nobody
but her and her husband

She was a really famous archeologist, known by all the adventures written about her 1000 trips she did
throughout her life.

However, she never wanted any of that popularity, leading her and her husband to isolate from society
in the jungle for their own good

Life surrounded by what she investigated was good, and with the company of her husband it was just
the perfect life.

No problems at all, but what they didn’t know is that that perfect life was going to change for the both
of them forever



Chapter 2




Today was a very special night for the both of them, it was the day when she proposed to her husband,



right in this place, at this hour, and it felt like the stars were at the same place as they always were.



However,...



A giant meteor crashed down the forest!!!



They went as fast as they could towards the place it landed. Nothing that strange has ever happened to
them before, and it could be a great opportunity for them in the name of archeology, but as soon as
they got there, they found the unexpected.



A baby was in the meteor



As strange as it was, the old woman couldn’t help but feel some sort of joy by the baby. She always
wanted to have a baby that she could take care of and extend the Starker family legacy. You never could
take the first step out of fear of your husband rejecting the idea, but when you saw the baby, you
couldn't help but see how happy the 3 of you would be together, you even thought of the name already
"Mark Starker" the meteor boy.

However.



Her husband grabbed her, he could see the joy in her eyes, but he knew that sooner or later that joy
would die forever. The old woman not understanding why he was taking her away from the meteor
baby, started screaming in panic and struggling to get out of there. He couldn’t let her take the baby, for
that baby was destined to die at some point.



The old woman stopped moving, she cried.































Chapter 3




A mysterious figure is seen on the distance, with eyes of shame, for leaving the meteor baby behind




















































































































The next day, the husband went back to the meteor hole, he was going to get rid of all the evidence of
what happened last day, even lying to his wife making her think that all that happened that day was just
a fever dream. However, when he got into the hole, nothing but a giant white screen was in there.

…

He never was the same after that unfortunate day
